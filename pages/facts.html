Interesting Animals

  • Animals do not fear the presence of humans.
  • Galápagos Marine Iguanas are the only ocean going lizards in the world.
  • The Galápagos Tortoise can live up to 150 years.

Island Environment

  • Geographic location results in 12 hours of sun every day.
  • 97% of the Galápagos is a national park.
  • Continous volcanic activity results in constant landscape shift.

Island Evolution

  • Researchers speculate they were formed around 3 to 4 million years ago which means that is a very young archipelago compared to Earth’s age which is around 4.45 billion years old.
  • The Pacific, Cocos and Nazca plates have resulted in 13 volcanic eruptions in the last 100 years.
